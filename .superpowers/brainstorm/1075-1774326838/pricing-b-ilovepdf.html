Common questions
Need something else? Contact us →
Do my files get uploaded anywhere?
Never. Every tool runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your files don't touch our servers — not even temporarily.
What's the difference between Day Pass and Pro?
Day Pass is a $1 one-time unlock for 24 hours — perfect if you have a batch to process today and don't need an account. Pro is $5/month (or $4/month billed annually) and adds 200MB files, full history, and priority support.
Can I cancel Pro any time?
Yes — cancel any time from your dashboard. No questions asked, no cancellation fees, no gotchas.
What counts as a "file" on the free tier?
3 files per tool per browser session. So you can merge 3 PDFs, compress 3 PDFs, and convert 3 PDFs — all free in the same session across different tools.
Is annual billing worth it?
If you use RizzPDF regularly, the annual plan saves you $12 a year — two months free. You're billed $48 upfront instead of $5/month. You can still cancel any time for a prorated refund.
